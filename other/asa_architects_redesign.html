About the Practice

Built on
craft &
conviction

ASA Architects is a South African practice founded on the belief that good architecture creates more equitable, beautiful, and enduring communities. Our work spans residential, commercial, urban design, and affordable housing — each project approached with the same rigour and sensitivity.

We collaborate closely with our clients to understand not just what they need today, but what their communities will require in the decades ahead.
